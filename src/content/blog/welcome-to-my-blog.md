Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm excited to finally have a space where I can share my thoughts, experiences, and learnings from over two decades in the tech industry.

## What to Expect

This blog will cover a variety of topics that I'm passionate about:

### Technical Deep Dives

I spend a lot of time exploring cloud native technologies, particularly eBPF, Kubernetes, and observability tools. Expect posts breaking down complex concepts into digestible pieces, with real-world examples and code snippets.

### Accessibility Advocacy

As a Deaf software engineer, accessibility isn't just a technical concern for me—it's personal. I'll be writing about making technology more inclusive, from practical implementation tips to broader discussions about accessibility culture in tech.

### Career Reflections

From QA engineer to software engineer, from physics student to tech veteran, my path has been anything but linear. I'll share lessons learned, career advice, and honest reflections on navigating this industry.

### Poetry and Personal Writing

Not everything will be code and tech. I'm a writer at heart, and sometimes that means poetry, personal essays, or just thoughts that don't fit neatly into a technical category.

## Why Now?

I've been in tech for over 20 years, and I've learned a lot along the way. But I've also benefited immensely from others who took the time to share their knowledge. This blog is my way of giving back.

Plus, I genuinely love to write. There's something satisfying about taking complex ideas and finding the right words to explain them clearly. It's not unlike writing good code—both require precision, clarity, and empathy for your audience.
